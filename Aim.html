Aim and Scope

The Nexus Journal of Research and Innovation is a leading international, peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to publishing cutting-edge research across all scientific disciplines.

Our Aim

The Nexus Journal aims to serve as a global platform for disseminating high-quality research that advances knowledge, promotes innovation, and addresses pressing challenges facing our world.

Scope of Research

We welcome original research articles, review papers, short communications, case studies, and technical notes contributing significant knowledge.
