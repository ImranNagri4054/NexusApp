Privacy Statement

Last updated: October 29, 2025

The Nexus Journal of Research and Innovation (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, submit manuscripts, or interact with our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Institutional affiliation and professional details
  • ORCID and other researcher identifiers
  • Research interests and academic credentials
  • Payment information for publication fees

Manuscript Information

When you submit a manuscript, we collect the content of your submission, author details, correspondence, and review comments.

Technical Information

We automatically collect IP addresses, browser type, device information, and usage data through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

  • Manuscript Processing: Managing peer review, editorial decisions, and publication
  • Communication: Sending notifications about submission status and updates
  • Indexing: Sharing article metadata with indexing services and databases
  • Analytics: Improving our services through usage analysis
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Published Articles

Author names, affiliations, ORCID IDs, and email addresses (for corresponding authors) are published with accepted articles.

Third-Party Service Providers

  • Peer review and manuscript systems
  • Indexing services (Scopus, Web of Science, etc.)
  • Digital preservation services (LOCKSS, Portico)
  • Payment processors
  • Technical service providers

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Security

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access control and authentication
  • Regular audits and monitoring
  • Staff training on data protection

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Access & Correction

Request access to or correction of your personal information.

Deletion

Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).

Restriction

Request restriction of processing in certain cases.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings

Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary for publication and legal compliance.

International Data Transfers

Information may be processed outside your country with appropriate safeguards.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time.
