Open Access Policy

The Nexus is committed to the principles of open access and the free dissemination of scholarly knowledge. In line with its mission to promote interdisciplinary research and academic collaboration, The Nexus provides immediate and unrestricted online access to all published articles.

All research articles published in The Nexus are freely available to readers worldwide without any subscription, registration, or payment barriers. Users are permitted to read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, or link to the full texts of articles, and to use them for any lawful purpose, provided that proper attribution is given to the original author(s) and the journal.

The Nexus follows the Gold Open Access model, ensuring that published content is permanently accessible on the journalâ€™s website. Authors retain the copyright of their work and grant the journal the right to publish and disseminate the article. Articles are published under a suitable Creative Commons license (e.g., CC BY), allowing reuse with appropriate citation.

By adopting an open access policy, The Nexus aims to enhance the global visibility, accessibility, and impact of published research, supporting academics, policymakers, and practitioners in advancing knowledge, informing practice, and fostering innovation across multiple disciplines.
